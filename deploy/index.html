Reviews

Anthony

3.5

4/8/22

Bought this to replace a toaster that had somehow lasted 30 years. It does the job and seems very well made. Like other reviewers, I have found that you need to start on a low setting. The toasting is otherwise very even. It does not have a bagel setting so will always toast both sides. Also does not have a "lift" so sometimes it is hard to grab the toast and remove. If you want lots of features, this is not the toaster for you. It does however have a setting for foods that are frozen at the start. I would recommend it for someone who plans to use it for toasting bread and not much else.

This machine is incredibly beautiful. That's why it gets five stars. And it toasts bread. Bonus. It has a bagel button where only one side of the bagel gets toasted. Perfect. Exciting. Hasn't done it since.Great design. Great color. Toasts!! I love this so much; looking at it helps in this pandemic!! Retail therapy at its best.

Steph

4.5

1/20/23

Still enjoying this toaster for the look and metal finish, but you just have to know that in terms of engineering this is a $30 toaster. It makes a cheap noise when the toast is done, and some of the time even sends the toast on the counter top because there is too much strength in the spring. I also had uneven toasting at first, I had to take a screwdriver and gently move the resistor wires away from the central part holding them because when the resistor touches the central part instead of having the usual bend, it doesn't heat up as much, you can see it by just looking into the toaster when it's on. Maybe the resistor doesn't get enough oxygen in that case or something. Was able to fix myself but kind of ridiculous for the price.
